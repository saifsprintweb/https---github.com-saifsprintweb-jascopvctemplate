Pipes

JASCO Pipes are manufactured according to ASTM Standards:

  • • SCH 40 PIPE - from 1/2" to 8"
  • • SCH 80 PIPE - from 1/2" to 8"
  • • CPCV SCH 80 PIPE - from 1/2" to 2"

Also, according to DIN Standards:

• All Classes (3-4-5) from 25mm to 225mm

JASCO UPVC and CPVC pipes are manufactured according to ASTM standards (American society for testing and materials). JASCO pipes are available under the brand name JASCO PIPES.

ASTM Standards:

ASTM D- 1784:

This specification covers Rigid PVC compound used for pipe extrusion.

ASTM D- 1785:

It covers the UPVC pipe requirements and test methods for schedule 40 and schedule 80.

ASTM F-441:

It covers schedule 80 CPVC pressure pipes.

ASTM D- 2665:

It covers schedule 40 DWV-UPVC pipes.

Note:

  • • JASCO CPVC pipes for hot water application are manufactured from 1/2 inch to 2 inch. These pipes are available in standard length of 6 meters.
  • • JASCO UPVC pipes are manufactured from 1/2 inch to 8 inch. These pipes are available in standard length of 6 meters. Other lengths are considered on customer's request.

Jaz Roof PVC Corrugated Sheet

Anti-corrosion

Soundproof Effect

Practically install the PVC corrugated sheets proved that even in heavy rain storms or inclement weather; it could effectively separate outward noise. Insulate sound 60% better than traditional color steel sheets.

Heat resistance

JAZ ROOF PVC Corrugated Sheet is excellent choice for roofing and wall application in high temperature areas. Its external layer reflect heat waves due to its heat resistance property which cause indoor temperature lower as compare to use of steel sheet.

Durability

JAZ ROOF PVC Corrugated Sheet which are used in the construction industry are coated with anti-scratch coating and ultraviolet protection. These ensurer that these sheet remain intact for a very long period of time even when they are exposed to adverse environmental conditions.

Easy to handle

Can be easily cut using hand or power tools, the lightweight but robust nature of the sheet makes it simple and easy to handle and install.

Lower maintenance cost

Because of all above advantages, the lifespan of JAZ ROOF PVC Corrugated Sheet is over 3 times than zinc sheet.
