| Overview

JASCO PVC

Al-Jazera Company for Plastic Industries (JASCO PVC) was established in 2016 under the umbrella of Al-Jazera Al-Arabia Group Ltd.

JASCO PVC aims to produce high-quality products by using the latest technology in the industry. We offer our customers a wide range of plastic products, including PVC Granules, PVC pipes, PVC fittings, and PVC corrugated sheets.

Quality is our identity; we believe in continuous improvement and innovation. JASCO PVC adopts the international quality management system ISO 9001:2015 to ensure that our products meet international quality standards.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision:

Al-Jazera Company for Plastic Industries (JASCO PVC) aims to be one of the leaders in the manufacturing of plastic pipes and fittings in Saudi Arabia in terms of "QUALITY" and "VARIETY." We also strive to be accredited by government agencies for all projects by introducing materials that prioritize the health of all family members and environmental safety.

Our Mission:

Our mission is to provide high-quality products and unsurpassed customer service. Through our success, we aim to achieve a fundamental shift towards environmental responsibility in the PVC industry, ensuring that JASCO PVC products are Healthy and Family Friendly.

TECHNICAL FEATURES

LEAD FREE (ZERO LEAD)

JASCO PVC Pipes & Fittings are made from high quality raw material that is totally free from heavy metals such as LEAD. It's completely 100% safe for potable water application and it is environment friendly. • Total heavy metal free. • Hygiene properties meet standard requirements. • Mechanical properties meet standard requirements. • More thermal stability.

CHEMICAL RESISTANCE

Since the main chain of the polymer is made by single bonds of carbon atoms, JASCO PVC Pipes & Fittings has excellent chemical resistance, it is resistant to most solutions of acids, alkalis, salts, and solutions which can be mixed with water.

FIRE PROOFING

JASCO PVC Pipes has inherently superior fire retarding properties due to its chlorine content, even in the absence of fire retardants. The ignition temperature of PVC is as high as 455°C and is a material with less risk for fire incidents since it is not ignited easily.

DURABILITY

JASCO PVC Pipes & Fittings are resistant to weathering, chemical rotting, corrosion, shock and abrasion. For durability of products, stabilizers are used in PVC pipes to prevent degradation of the polymer during the extrusion process and storage of the pipes before they are buried in the ground.

MECHANICAL STABILITY

JASCO PVC Pipes & Fittings have superior mechanical stability, with high resistance to pressure, it has incredible mechanical stability, which means it won't deform under pressure, so you can trust it to deliver reliable performance time and time again.

IMPACT STRENGTH

JASCO PVC Pipes & Fittings have great impact strength over their corresponding cast iron or clay pipes, which cut losses arising from the breakage due to mishandling during transportation, installation etc.
